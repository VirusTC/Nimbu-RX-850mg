# Chemistry, Manufacturing, and Controls (CMC) Botanical Monograph

**Project Tracking Code:** Nimbu-RX-850mg  
**Botanical Source:** *Citrus aurantiifolia* (Organic Lime)  
**Regulatory Blueprint:** FDA Botanical Drug Development Framework (21 CFR Part 312)

## 1. Botanical Raw Material & Farming Controls
To ensure strict batch-to-batch consistency under federal oversight, the raw materials are managed through agricultural and botanical parameters:
* **Farmed Source Integrity:** Active crops are harvested exclusively from organic, registered agricultural sites utilizing verified soil standards.
* **Botanical Authentication:** Genomic and morphological profiling verifies the genus and species as 100% *Citrus aurantiifolia*.
* **Harvest Controls:** Whole fruit collections are timestamped and transferred directly to climate-regulated environments to preserve volatile monoterpenes and prevent the degradation of organic acids.

The final manufactured crystalline output must fulfill the following quality benchmarks prior to clinical allocation:

* **Moisture Content:** Maintained at $\leq$ 2.5% via validated vacuum metrics to guarantee biological stability.
* **Residual Contamination:** Absolute zero presence of industrial solvent fractions or added mineral salts.
