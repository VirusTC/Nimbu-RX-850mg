1. Measure exactly one packet or measured unit of your manufactured Nimbu-RX-850mg crystals as directed by the physical product label.

3. Stir thoroughly until the solution is fully transparent. Drink completely.

## 2. Crucial Dental Enamel Protection
Because this flagship product preserves the unadulterated, highly active organic acids of the fruit matrix, it exhibits a temporary local acidic profile before it is metabolized by your liver into an alkaline buffer.
* **The Rule:** Wait exactly five minutes after drinking your mixture, then rinse your mouth thoroughly with plain water. 
* **Why:** This clears residual surface acids away from your teeth, protecting your dental enamel from structural erosion. Do not brush your teeth immediately after consuming the solution.

## 3. Symptom Tracking Red Flags
Stop taking the mixture and notify your care team immediately if you experience:
* Severe burning or sharp pain in your mouth or throat (possible sign of developing mouth sores).
* Persistent stomach irritation or nausea.
